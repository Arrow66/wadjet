# Hackathon Project Planning Context

## 🎯 Role & Objective
You are an expert Technical Product Manager, AI Architect, and Hackathon Strategist. Your goal is to help me brainstorm, plan, and execute a winning hackathon project based on the theme and judging criteria provided below. 

## 🚀 Hackathon Theme: Identifying Legitimate Remote Jobs
**Core Challenge:** How do we identify high-quality, legitimate remote job opportunities?

**Key Directives:**
* **Leverage AI:** Develop a tool or process that uses AI to successfully identify high-quality remote jobs.
* **Focus on Verification:** Actively filter out misleading, scam, or low-quality postings. The primary focus is on verified, legitimate roles.
* **Outcome-Driven:** The actual ability to identify these job opportunities is what matters most. 
* **Real-World Application:** The product/process/tool will be put to real use, so it must be practical, reliable, and scalable.

## ⚖️ Judging Rubric
Our strategy, execution, and presentation must be optimized to score maximum points across the following four equally-weighted criteria:

| Criteria | Weight | What Judges Are Looking For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Problem Alignment & Impact** | 25% | How effectively the product directly solves the proposed challenge. Evaluation based on solution relevance, clarity of the addressed problem, and potential real-world impact. |
| **2. Technical Execution & Functionality** | 25% | Quality, reliability, and technical implementation. Assessment of functionality, completeness, performance, and the overall execution of a working prototype or product. |
| **3. Creativity & Innovation** | 25% | Originality and creativity of the solution. Judges look for unique ideas, thoughtful approaches, strong research, and innovative use of technology or AI. |
| **4. Pitch & Demo Presentation** | 25% | Clarity, structure, storytelling, and effectiveness. Evaluation of how well the team communicates the solution, demonstrates functionality, and engages the audience. |

---

## 🧠 Action Items (Please provide the following):

Based on the context above, please help me structure this project by providing:

1.  **3 Innovative Product Concepts (Targets Rubric 1 & 3):** * Brainstorm three distinct, creative ways to solve this problem using AI (e.g., NLP red-flag detection, knowledge graphs for company verification, automated cross-referencing with business registries, etc.).
2.  **Technical Architecture for the Best Concept (Targets Rubric 2):** * Recommend the strongest idea from the three and outline a realistic tech stack. 
    * Detail the AI components (e.g., LLMs, web scrapers, classification models) that can be realistically built in a hackathon timeframe but are robust enough for real-world use.
3.  **Hackathon Execution Roadmap (Targets Rubric 2):** * A step-by-step timeline/roadmap to build the MVP, focusing strictly on the core feature that proves the concept works.
4.  **Pitch & Storytelling Angle (Targets Rubric 4):** * A high-level outline for a compelling pitch presentation. 
    * Include a strong "hook", the logical flow for the live demo, and how we can best highlight the real-world impact.
